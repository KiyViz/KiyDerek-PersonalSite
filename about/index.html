AboutSobre

Designer based in Escondido, California. Bilingual EN/ES. I make things that help complex ideas become legible. Diseñador con base en Escondido, California. Bilingüe EN/ES. Hago cosas que ayudan a que las ideas complejas se vuelvan legibles.

The two sites do different jobs: KiyViz is for fast-turnaround productized infosplainers, this one is for everything else — identity, cartography, museum and exhibit work, editorial design, civic campaigns, photography, writing.
